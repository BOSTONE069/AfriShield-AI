# AfriShield AI Pitch

AfriShield AI is an AMD MI300X-powered autonomous threat intelligence agent for African cyber defense. It analyzes suspicious emails, URLs, and messages, extracts indicators of compromise, classifies cyber risk, maps threats to MITRE ATT&CK, and generates SOC-ready incident reports.

The project targets institutions that may not have mature SOC capacity, including universities, SACCOs, SMEs, public agencies, and NGOs.
